Visit Salvador!

Come and enjoy the lively Afro-Brazilian culture, rich history and beautiful natural landscape that Salvador has to offer!

Top three places to enjoy in Salvador:

    Pelourinho

    It is the heart of Bahia, the center of African culture in Brazil and the best attraction in the historic center of Salvador.

    Farol da Barra

    The most famous lighthouse in the world brings together history, culture and leisure in one place.

    Praia do Flamengo

    Far from the bustling city center, it is perfect for the traveler to have fun and relax.
